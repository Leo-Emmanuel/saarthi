# Math Notations - Quick Reference

## 1. Addition
Say: "2 plus 3"  
Result: 2 + 3

## 2. Subtraction
Say: "5 minus 2"  
Result: 5 - 2

## 3. Multiplication
Say: "4 times 5"  
Result: 4 × 5

## 4. Division
Say: "10 divided by 2"  
Result: 10 ÷ 2

## 5. Negative Numbers
Say: "negative 5"  
Result: -5

## 6. Squared
Say: "x squared"  
Result: x²

## 7. Cubed
Say: "x cubed"  
Result: x³

## 8. Power
Say: "x to the power 4"  
Result: x⁴

## 9. Square Root
Say: "square root of 9"  
Result: √9

## 10. Cube Root
Say: "cube root of 8"  
Result: ∛8

## 11. Nth Root
Say: "4 root of 81"  
Result: ⁴√81

## 12. Fraction
Say: "3 over 4"  
Result: 3/4

## 13. Root with Fraction
Say: "square root of 29 over 2"  
Result: √29/2

## 14. Equation
Say: "x equals 5"  
Result: x = 5

## 15. Quadratic
Say: "x squared plus 3x minus 5 equals 0"  
Result: x² + 3x - 5 = 0

## 16. Plus or Minus
Say: "x equals negative 3 plus or minus square root of 29 over 2"  
Result: x = (-3 ± √29)/2

## 17. Integration (Simple)
Say: "integral 2x dx"  
Result: ∫2x dx

## 18. Integration (With Limits)
Say: "integral from 0 to 1 of x squared dx"  
Result: ∫₀¹ x² dx

## 19. Summation
Say: "sum from n equals 1 to 5 of n squared"  
Result: Σ(n=1 to 5) n²

## 20. Derivative
Say: "d by dx of x squared"  
Result: d/dx(x²)

## 21. Sine
Say: "sin of x"  
Result: sin(x)

## 22. Cosine
Say: "cos of x"  
Result: cos(x)

## 23. Tangent
Say: "tan of x"  
Result: tan(x)

## 24. Logarithm
Say: "log of x"  
Result: ln(x)

## 25. Logarithm Base
Say: "log base 2 of 8"  
Result: log₂(8)

## 26. Infinity
Say: "infinity"  
Result: ∞

## 27. Subscript
Say: "a sub 1"  
Result: a₁

## Voice Commands

Say: "new step"  
Result: Creates new step

Say: "undo"  
Result: Undo last action

Say: "redo"  
Result: Redo last action
